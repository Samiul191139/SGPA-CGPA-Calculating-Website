Examination System Overview

The grades for each course are determined based on performance in first-term, mid-term, final exams, quizzes, attendance, projects, and other activities. The breakdown of weights is as follows:

  • First Term Examination: 20%
  • Mid-Term Examination: 20%
  • Quizzes (minimum 10): 5%
  • Attendance: 5%
  • Assignments/Projects/Presentations: 15%
  • Final Examination: 35%
